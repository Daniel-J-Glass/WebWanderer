Polymerized Creatures

Welcome to the world of polymerized creatures, where nodules grant power to those who seek it. The creatures in this world are unlike anything you've ever seen before. They're made of a special polymer that allows them to change shape and color. The nodules, on the other hand, are small, glowing orbs that seem to have a life of their own. They float around the creatures, granting them incredible powers.

As you explore this world, you'll encounter all kinds of creatures, from tiny, harmless creatures to massive, terrifying beasts. Some will be friendly, while others will be hostile. It's up to you to decide whether to fight or flee.
